About Pixi Prints

Welcome to Pixi Prints! This shop is a self-owned business that started as a creative passion and grew into a small business built on a love for art and of course dogs. Every design for the prints and stickers is hand drawn by me and then colored and polished digitally. All jewelry is handmade each day and the coolest part is that no two pieces are exactly alike. Pixi Prints is a growing business offering a variety of products including art prints, stickers, and jewelry designed to stand out and bring a smile to customers. For updates on the shop you can follow @PixiPrints on Instagram and X. New merchandise drops every month, so be sure to check back often. Thank you for visiting and supporting our small business.
